About Me

I believe that life is about learning as much as we can, so if a person feels like there is no more room to learn then they are no longer living. This is the motto that I have followed throughout my whole life and it has allowed me to learn from any opportunity I've ever been presented. The reason I became interested in software development is because I've always had a passion for building things. When I was younger I used to build all sorts of toys with random pieces of metal and plastics that I would find in my town. I also got involved with a lot of art mediums at a young age, especially sculpting.

Eventually, I decided to take both my love for art sculpting and technology by learning how to write code. I found it to be very similar to making a sculpture with my hands, but instead now I build with programming languages and a computer. It is the most challenging and rewarding knowledge I've ever had the pleasure to be introduced to and work towards mastering. I am excited to be able to join a development team soon and continue to expand my knowledge. I am never fearful of change or being taught something by a higher skilled individual, which is great in the ever changing technology field.

A game app inspired by Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda series. The user clicks on rupee's with different values to attempt to match the random number. If the user matches they win, if they go over the random number they lose.

A trivia app insipired by games from the 70's to the present. It consists of questions with multiple choice answers that the user has to answer before time expires. At the end it displays the user's results and also a reset button to start the app again.

Gif Generator

App that incorporates Google Firebase to host arrival and departure data. App retrieves and manipulates this information with Moment.js. App provides up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station.
