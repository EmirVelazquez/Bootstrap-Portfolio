About Me

Hello, my name is Emir Velazquez and I am an aspiring developer. My goal is to join a company that needs excellent individuals to depend on. I became interested in development because I've always had a passion for building things. When I was younger I used to build all sorts of toys and also got involved with a lot of art mediums especially sculpting. Another thing I really love is technology, especially computers and video games.

After working full time and trying to still further my higher education, I decided to take both of my loves for art sculpting and technology by learning how to write code. I find it very similar to making a sculpture but instead with a programming language, it is challenging and rewarding. I am excited to be able to join a development team soon and continue to expand my knowledge.
