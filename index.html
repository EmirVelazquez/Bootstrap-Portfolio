About Me

Hello, my name is Emir Velazquez and I am an aspiring Developer. My goal is to join a company that needs excellent individuals to depend on. I became interested in development because I've always had a passion for building things. When I was younger I used to build all sorts of toys and also got involved with a lot of art. One of the main art mediums I enjoyed working with was Sculpture. Another thing I really love is technology, especially computers and video games.

After working full time and trying to still further my higher education, I decided to take both of my loves for art sculpting and technology. No better place to do that then by learning how to write code. It is basically like making a sculpture using programming languages and I find it very challenging and rewarding. I am excited to be able to join a develpment team soon and continue to expand my knowledge. Thank you for taking the time to read my introduction.
